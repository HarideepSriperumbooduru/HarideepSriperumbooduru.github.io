TRIAL BY MEDIA

World Today

Today’s world is run by data and information. Data and Information plays a crucial role in decision making almost in every sector, category, and company. We humans are so dependent on data that not even a day goes by without us analysing some kind of data or information for various reasons such as for fun and knowledge, to make decisions, or to simply share it with others. This constant need for consuming information at every step of our life has put us in the path of destruction. So, there is a dire need to segregate factual information from misinformation or opinions for people to make decisions with clarity.

A fact is a statement that can be proven true or false. An opinion is an expression of a person’s feelings that cannot be proven. Opinions can be based on emotions and sometimes they are meant to deliberately mislead others. An opinion is something that can’t be vetted or authenticated. In today’s scientific world we can’t take any information on its face value, so it's time for us to consider opinions as false news until and unless they can be corroborated and authenticated.

With ever growing social connectivity with all the mediums for us to connect with the world sitting right where we are, just by a click of a button on our mobile, the flow of information has never been so fluid in human history. False news travels 7 times faster than factual news and reaches farther and deeper into societies across countries with much more ease than factual information. Humans have figured out a way to hack the people’s brain for profit and how to push those cognitive buttons in the brain to make opinions look as facts.
