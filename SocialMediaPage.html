MIT Media Lab research on Twitter

An extensive research conducted by MIT media lab on twitter database to take in to count all the tweets that have been tweeted since its inception in 2006 made it clear that false news travels farther, faster, and deeper than factual information in every category of information by an order of magnitude.According to research false news on twitter is 70% more likely to be retweeted by any given individual than the true factual information and also that true stories take about six times as long to reach 1,500 people as it does for false stories to reach the same number of people.
