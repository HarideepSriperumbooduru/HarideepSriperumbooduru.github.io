STATUS-QUO OF SOCIAL MEDIA

Why social media

Social media has provided a medium to publish opinions or rather false news that look as plausible as what used to be considered authentic factual information and that to without any authentication. Every person in today's stressful world needs a medium to express thier views and opinions to free thier mind. In today's digital age people are prefering social media platforms as they don't require us to corroborate something that we have expressed. Almost all the social media platforms are free of cost and they make it a point to protect our personal information if, we don't want anybody to see it.

We as humans identify a group of consistent features in data that make it look likely that the information given is true, false, or even make you look at it in the first place. Whenever we come across some information which has all these consistent features of factual information we tend not to authenticate the information before we consume or share it. The extensive research of MIT media lab on propagation of false news vs facts has made it clear that false news is overwhelmingly novel as compared to factual information.
