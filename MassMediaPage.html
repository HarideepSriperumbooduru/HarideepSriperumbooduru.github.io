MASS MEDIA INFLUENCE

High Publicity Court Cases

Cases such as sushant singh rajput’s death to which the entire nation is emotionally connected gives a perfect opportunity for the media to push their opinions on the general public’s consensus. The sad part of these situations is that any democratic government has to spend enormous resources to verify the allegations from the general public because of trial by media. Twists and turns occurred in the case because of media swaying the general public from the truth were
  • Death vs suicide
  • Bollywood vs Outsider
  • Arab mafia money laundering
  • Maharashtra government vs Bihar government
  • CBI vs NCB
  • Kangana vs Maharashtra government
The case had long back taken turn from the fact that some body was murdered in cold blood to business, political, and personal benefits of media houses, political parties, and individuals.

Political Agenda

Almost every other mass media company is directly owned or associated with some politician in India which makes it impossible for the general public to get unbiased information regarding the ongoing events. Political parties use these media houses to label something as false news that might not have good reflection on the party. It is a major political strategy for dampening the value of that information. If the regional situation is considered in andhra pradesh two big media houses are directly associated with the ruling and opposition parties. This situation almost always leads to trial by media in any important case against either the ruling or opposition party leading to persuading the general public to side with the political party’s agenda even before the trial happens in the court.

Political Propaganda

In a country such as China and North korea where the mass media companies are directly owned by the governments there is no meaning for freedom of press.These media companies are widely used to further the propaganda of ruling governments and keep its citizens under control feeding them information which the government sees as necessary. Global Times, an official media house of China's ruling party supports all of the deliberate misleading actions done by chinese military at India- China border as part of it’s peace making. It has also posted an article saying 72% of China's citizens agree to go to war with India propagating the it’s government’s opinion to sway general public consensus.
